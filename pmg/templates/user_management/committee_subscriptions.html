How To Subscribe

Send an email to subscribe@pmg.org.za providing the following info:

  • Contact person
  • Job Title
  • Organization
  • Organization's VAT number
  • Work phone
  • Postal Address
  • Email Address
  • Committees you wish to subscribe to
We will then email you a Statement / Tax invoice.
